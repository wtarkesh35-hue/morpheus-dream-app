I was flying over a dark ocean at night. Below me, a lighthouse was spinning slowly. I kept trying to land on it but every time I got close it would sink beneath the waves.

✦ Jungian Interpretation

The Lighthouse of the Self

Flight in Jungian symbolism represents the transcendence of the ego — a liberation from earthly constraints and the desire of the psyche to rise above its limitations. The dark ocean beneath you is the unconscious itself: vast, uncharted, and full of depth that the waking mind cannot access directly.

The lighthouse is your guiding self — the part of you that seeks to illuminate the unknown. Its spinning motion suggests this inner guide is active and present, but its sinking signals a fear that clarity may be just beyond your reach. You are drawn to understanding, but something pulls that understanding away before you can land on it.

Flight → Liberation
Ocean → The Unconscious
Lighthouse → Inner Guide
Sinking → Elusive Truth
